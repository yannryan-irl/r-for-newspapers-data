"DOCK  COMMITTEE.-YESTERDAY  .--.--_  The  usual  weekly  meeting  of  the  dock  committee  was  held  at  one  o'clock,  Mr.  Charles  Turner  in  the  chair.  The  other  gentlemen  present  were,  Messrs.  Shand,  Palmer,  Robinson,  Brocklebank,  Chaloner,  Rounthwaite,  James  Holme,  Barber,  Holden,  Mondell,  Langsdale,  Anderson,  Hubback,  Rankin,  Evans.  A  report  was  read  of  the  recent  fire  which  took  place  in  the  Brunswick  dockyard."
