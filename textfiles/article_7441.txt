"LATEST  INTELLIGENCE.  SECOND  EDITION.  THE  CRIMEA.  DESPATCH  FROM  GEN.  CODRINGTON  The  Gazette  of  last  night  contains  a  despatch  from  General  Sir  W.  Codrington,  dated  Sebastopol,  Dec.  15th,  but  it  merely  alludes  to  the  report  of  Dr.  Hall,  Inspector-General  of  Hospitals,  which  announces  that  the  general  state  of  health  of  the  army  continues  good.  The  deaths  during  the  week  were  thirty-seven,  and  in  the  previous  week  thirty-eight."
