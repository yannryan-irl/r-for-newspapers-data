"SHANGHAE,  Sept.  22.  The  Panic,  from  Liverpool,  which  got  ashore  below  Woosung,  in  a  typhoon,  floated  last  night,  after  discharging  about  800  packages  into  boats,  and  is  expected  up  here  to-morrow.  The  ship,  apparently,  has  not  sustained  the  least  damage."
