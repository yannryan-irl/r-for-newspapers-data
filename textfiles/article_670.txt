"MANCHESTER  ROBBERY  OF  A  WATCH  -On  Saturday,  at  the  Town  Hall,  Salford,  a  female  named  Hannah  Morrison,  was  brought  up  on  a  charge  of  illegally  pawning  a  watch,  the  property  of  a  person  named  Hibbert.  It  appears,  that  on  the  19th  ultimo  Hibbert  was  in  the  streets  asleep  under  the  influence  of  liquor,  when  he  awoke,  his  watch  was  gone,  and  he  gave  information  to  the  police.  On  inquiry,  it  was  ascertained  that  the  prisoner  had  pawned  the  watch  on  Saturday  week  with  Mr.  Kenyon,  Gateside-street.  The  prisoner  was  ordered  to  pay  £3,  the  value  of  the  watch,  and  to  be  final  4Cs.  and  costs,  or,  in  default,  to  be  imprisoned  for  two  months  with  hard  labour.
