BOROUGH  OF  LIVERPOOL,  TO  WIT.—GILBERT  HENDERSON,  ESQ.,  Recorder.—The  Court'  of  Quarter  Sessions  of  the  Peace  for  the  Borough  will  be  held  in  the  Sessions'  House,  Chapel-streeet,  in  the  said  Borough,  on  Tuesday,  the  13th  Day  of  December  instant,  at  Ten  o'clock  in  the  forenoon.
