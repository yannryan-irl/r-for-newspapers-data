"PENINSULAR  MAIL.  The  Peninsular  Mail  steamer  Iberia  arrived  at  Southampton  at  a  late  hour  on  Saturday  night,  bringing  twenty  five  passengers  and  one  thousaud  five  hundred  and  thirty  pounds.  The  dates  from  .Gibralter  are  to  the  Bth;  Cadiz  the  flth;  Lisbon  the  10th;  and  Vigo  the  12th  inst.  The  Iberia  was  detained  three  days  at  Gibralter  in  quarantine."
