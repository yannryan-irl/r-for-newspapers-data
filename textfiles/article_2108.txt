I  believe  that  no  part  of  the  community  is  more  interested  in  this  question  than  yourselves;  not  only  from  the  central  position  of  your  ward,  but  on  account  of  its  possessing  so  many  places  of  public  resort,  viz  :  —the  Theatres,the  Concert-hall,  St.  John's  Market,  the  Railway  Station,  and  St.  George's  Hall.
