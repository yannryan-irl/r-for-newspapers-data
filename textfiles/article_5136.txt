"SINGAPORE,  Oct.  17.  The  Ignis  Paton:l,  arrived  here,  brings  news  from  Port  Philip,  Sept  6,  timber,  pepper,  and  other  articles  had  fallen  considerably;  wooden  houses  however,  were  commanding  pretty  favorable  prices.  The  C.  S.  Forbes,  has  arrived  here,  from  New  South  Wa  es,  with  intelligence  from  Sydney.  to  Sept  5."
