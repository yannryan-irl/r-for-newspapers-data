"BURY.  At  Bury,  there  is  no  change  since  Friday.  The  operatives  have  had  open-air  meetings  every  day,  and  on  Tuesday  morning,  about  5,000  of  them  mustered  in  Union-square,  and  after  forming  in  procession,  proceeded  in  the  direction  of  Heywood  anl  Rochdale.  No  interview  has  been  sought  with  their  employers  since  the  closing  of  the  mills."
