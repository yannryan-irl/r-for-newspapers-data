"TO  OUR  MANCHESTER  SUBSCRIBERS.  Our  Manchester  friends  will  please  observe,  that  our  Publishing-office  in  that  town  will,  on  Monday,  December  12th,  be  removed  from  9,  Princess  -street,  to  No.  91,  MARKET-STREET.  On  and  after  the  same  date,  the  NORTHERN  DAILY  TIMES  will  be  delivered  in  Manchester  every  morning  at  Seven  o'clock.  CHARLES  WILLMER,  PUBLISHER.  Dec.  9th,  1853."
